MedTracker v1.0.0 - Initial Release
- Add medications with name, dosage, frequency, and reminder times
- Multiple daily reminders via AlarmManager
- Mark doses as taken/skipped
- Refill tracking with remaining pills count and low-supply alerts
- Drug interaction warnings (built-in database of common interactions)
- Calendar view showing medication schedule
- History log (when taken, when missed)
- Export history as JSON
- Dark theme, Material Design 3
- About section with version, update check (GitHub API), share button
- Boot receiver to reschedule alarms after restart
